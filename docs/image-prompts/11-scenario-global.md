# 11 — 场景插画：出海与国际业务

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"出海与国际业务" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-global.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI powering international business operations. Scene elements:

- A stylized globe or world map in the center (geometric/low-poly style, not photorealistic), with connection lines to multiple regions
- Document icons in different languages/scripts floating around the globe (Chinese, English, Arabic — suggested by different text block patterns, not actual text)
- An amber AI agent translating/converting documents: one document goes in on the left, multiple localized versions come out on the right
- Currency exchange indicators: ¥ → $ → € with small exchange arrows
- A news monitoring feed on one side showing flagged items (representing sentiment monitoring)

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents and the globe highlights, teal (#0D9488) for connection lines and positive indicators, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style. The image should convey global reach, multilingual capability, and cross-border intelligence. No realistic people.

## 用途说明
代表"海外尽调、多语种处理、跨境票据审核、舆情监控"的出海场景。

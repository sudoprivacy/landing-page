# 19 — 底部 CTA 背景图

> **用途：** "预约演示" CTA 区块的背景
> **尺寸：** 1920×600
> **文件名：** cta-bg.png

## Prompt

A wide, cinematic abstract background on dark charcoal (#1a1a1a). Compositionally similar to the hero background (01) but with a different focal pattern to avoid repetition.

Elements:
- A large, semi-transparent amber (#F59E0B) isometric cube structure in the center-right, composed of thin wireframe lines at about 10-15% opacity — echoing the SudoClaw logo but much larger and more abstract
- Subtle particle dots scattered across the canvas in amber and teal, suggesting data flow or network activity
- A gentle horizontal light streak across the middle (amber gradient, very subtle) suggesting forward momentum
- Darker at the edges, slightly lighter in the center to create a natural vignette that draws the eye to where the CTA text will be placed

No text, no logos. This is purely atmospheric — text and buttons will be overlaid in HTML. The overall mood: confident, forward-looking, warm technology.

底部"预约演示"区块的背景。上面会叠加大标题和 CTA 按钮。需要比 Hero 背景更"暖"一点，有收尾感。

# 13 — 场景插画：人力与行政

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"人力与行政" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-hr.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI streamlining HR and administrative workflows. Scene elements:

- A resume screening conveyor: a stack of resumes flowing in from the left, an amber AI agent quickly scanning and sorting them into "recommended" (teal pile) and "review later" (gray pile) stacks, with match scores floating above each
- An onboarding workflow track on the right: a new employee icon moving through connected steps — information collection → contract signing → account setup → training assignment, each step with a green checkmark as it completes
- A digital assistant (amber hexagon) guiding the new employee through each step via chat bubbles
- Progress indicators and completion percentages

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents and scoring highlights, teal (#0D9488) for completed/positive indicators, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style. Clean and efficient, conveying automation of repetitive HR tasks. No realistic people — use simple geometric shapes for person icons.

## 用途说明
代表"简历筛选评分、入职流程自动化"的 HR 场景。

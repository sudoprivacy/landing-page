# 05 — 场景插画：客户服务

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"客户服务" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-service.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI-powered customer service automation. Scene elements:

- A central hub/dashboard screen showing a customer service control panel with chat bubbles, ticket queues, and status indicators
- Multiple chat conversation threads flowing in from the left (representing customer inquiries)
- Small amber hexagonal cube agents (AI workers) sorting and responding to different threads simultaneously
- A quality monitoring panel on the right showing green checkmarks and alert indicators
- Some threads have a small "transfer to human" arrow going to a person icon, representing escalation

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents, teal (#0D9488) for resolved/successful indicators, a touch of red for alerts, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style, no realistic people — humans represented as simple circles or abstract shapes. Clean, professional enterprise aesthetic.

## 用途说明
代表"AI 7×24 自动客服 + 全量质检 + 智能升级"的场景。

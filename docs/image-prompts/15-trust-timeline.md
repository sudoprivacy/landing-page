# 15 — 渐进信任四阶段图

> **用途：** "信任是渐进建立的" 区块的时间轴配图
> **尺寸：** 1600×500
> **文件名：** trust-timeline.png

## Prompt

A horizontal timeline infographic on a dark background (#1a1a1a) showing four stages of progressive AI trust and autonomy:

Stage 1 (leftmost): "Human Confirms" — A person icon with a raised hand, next to a small AI cube. The person is clearly in control, the AI is small and suggesting. Icon: hand + checkmark. Color: mostly white/gray, subtle amber.

Stage 2: "Semi-Auto" — The person and AI cube are similar size. The AI handles some tasks independently (shown by a split path: easy tasks go through AI directly, complex ones route to human). Icon: half-gear half-hand. Color: more amber emerging.

Stage 3: "Full Automation" — The AI cube is prominent, the person is in the background monitoring. Only a thin "red line" path goes to human for critical decisions. Icon: interlocking gears. Color: dominant amber.

Stage 4 (rightmost): "Custom Model" — A diamond/star shape (representing the enterprise's unique AI model). It glows — this is the final evolution. Below: text suggesting "your proprietary AI asset." Icon: diamond with sparkle. Color: bright amber with a teal glow.

Connecting the four stages: a gradient line progressing from white → amber → bright amber, with subtle pulse dots moving along it. Below each stage: a single-line description in Chinese (rendered as placeholder text blocks). The overall progression communicates growing trust and capability.

## 用途说明
传达"不要求企业一步到位信任 AI，而是分四个阶段渐进建立"。这对中国企业客户非常重要——他们需要看到控制权始终在自己手里。

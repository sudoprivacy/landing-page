# 07 — 场景插画：财务与合规

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"财务与合规" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-accounting.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI automating financial reconciliation and compliance. Scene elements:

- A large spreadsheet/table floating at an angle, with rows being automatically highlighted and matched by small amber AI agents
- One agent cross-referencing two documents (showing a diff/comparison view with green matches and red mismatches)
- A compliance checklist on the right side with most items checked off in teal
- Currency symbols (¥, $, €) floating subtly in the background, suggesting multi-currency handling
- A clock/calendar icon suggesting automated scheduling

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents and highlights, teal (#0D9488) for success/match indicators, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style. Professional and precise, conveying accuracy and automation. No realistic people.

## 用途说明
代表"自动对账、合规分析、报销审核、多币种处理"的财务场景。

# 17 — 产品矩阵插画：Nexus OS 架构图

> **用途：** 产品矩阵区块，Nexus OS 卡片的背景/配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** product-nexus.png

## Prompt

An elegant architectural diagram of a three-layer AI operating system called "Nexus OS," shown in isometric view on a warm cream background (#FAF9F0).

Three horizontal layers stacked vertically with slight separation:

Bottom layer (foundation): "Extension / Plugin" — shown as a row of modular blocks (puzzle pieces or plug icons) in teal (#0D9488), representing connectors to external systems (ERP, CRM, databases)

Middle layer: "Skill" — shown as a collection of specialized knowledge cards or skill badges in amber (#F59E0B), representing packaged expertise and rules

Top layer: "Agent" — shown as assembled AI worker cubes (amber hexagons from the SudoClaw logo style), representing the complete AI employees that combine skills and extensions

Vertical connection lines between layers show how Agents use Skills which access Extensions. A shield icon on the side represents the built-in privacy computing engine. An "Apache 2.0" open-source badge in the corner.

Style: Clean, geometric, layered architecture diagram. Professional and technical but not cluttered. The three layers should be immediately distinguishable.

## 用途说明
展示 Nexus OS 的三层架构（Plugin/Extension + Skill + Agent），让技术决策者理解系统设计。

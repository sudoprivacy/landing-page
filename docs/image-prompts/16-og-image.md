# 16 — OG 社交分享图

> **用途：** 微信分享、Twitter Card、LinkedIn 预览图
> **尺寸：** 1200×630（社交分享标准尺寸）
> **文件名：** og-image.png

## Prompt

A professional social sharing card image for "SudoClaw" — an enterprise AI workforce platform.

Layout:
- Dark background (#1a1a1a) with subtle geometric hexagonal pattern (very low opacity, 5-8%)
- Left side (40%): The SudoClaw logo — an amber (#F59E0B) isometric hexagonal cube, large and prominent
- Right side (60%):
  - Main text (large, white, bold): "SudoClaw"
  - Subtitle (smaller, white 70% opacity): "企业硅基劳动力平台"
  - Tagline (even smaller, amber): "9 个岗位 · 8 套系统 · 6 周上线"
- Bottom: A thin amber horizontal line as accent

The overall feel: premium, enterprise, trustworthy. Think of it as a digital business card. Clean typography, strong contrast, minimal elements. The amber cube logo should be the visual anchor.

## 用途说明
当有人在微信群或朋友圈分享 SudoClaw 网站链接时，预览图就是这张。需要在小缩略图（约 200×105）尺寸下仍然清晰可识别。

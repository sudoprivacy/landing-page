# 12 — 场景插画：知识管理与决策支持

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"知识管理与决策支持" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-knowledge.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI as an enterprise knowledge and decision support system. Scene elements:

- A large knowledge graph/network in the center: interconnected nodes of different sizes representing documents, decisions, expertise areas, connected by thin lines
- An amber AI agent acting as a librarian/search engine, pulling specific nodes out of the graph in response to a search query
- On one side: scattered messy documents (emails, chat logs, files) being organized — flowing into the knowledge graph in a neat structure
- On the other side: a clean executive dashboard showing a summary report with key metrics and a recommendation badge
- A calendar with highlighted dates (representing scheduled reminders and deadline tracking)

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents and important nodes, teal (#0D9488) for connection lines and the knowledge graph, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style. The image should convey "turning organizational chaos into structured, searchable intelligence." No realistic people.

## 用途说明
代表"知识沉淀、经验不随人走、跨系统数据检索、智能提醒"的管理场景。

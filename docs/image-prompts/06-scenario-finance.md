# 06 — 场景插画：金融与保险

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"金融与保险" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-finance.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of privacy-preserving AI in financial services. Scene elements:

- Two distinct zones (left and right) representing two institutions (e.g., a bank and a telecom), each with their own data vault/server icon
- In the center: a secure computation zone shown as a translucent amber shield/hexagon where data from both sides connects without crossing boundaries — representing "data usable but invisible"
- Small lock icons on the data paths between the vaults and the central zone
- Output: a risk score/model result emerging from the top of the central zone
- Subtle mathematical formula fragments and probability curves floating in the background

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for the privacy computation zone, teal (#0D9488) for the data flows, dark charcoal for the institutional zones, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. The overall feel should communicate security, trust, and cross-institutional collaboration. No realistic people. Clean geometric style.

## 用途说明
代表"隐私计算 + 联邦建模 + 数据可用不可见"的金融场景。这是数牍的核心技术优势。

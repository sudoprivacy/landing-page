# 08 — 场景插画：运营与供应链

> **用途：** 场景案例区块，"运营与供应链" 分类的配图
> **尺寸：** 800×600
> **文件名：** scenario-supply-chain.png

## Prompt

Minimalist isometric illustration of AI managing supply chain operations. Scene elements:

- A central operations dashboard with inventory levels displayed as bar charts
- A conveyor belt or flow path connecting: supplier (factory icon) → warehouse (box stacks) → delivery (truck icon)
- Small amber hexagonal AI agents monitoring each stage: one flagging a delayed shipment (red alert), one updating inventory counts, one generating a report
- Notification bells and alert indicators floating above problem areas
- A timeline bar at the bottom showing automated daily scheduling

Color palette: Amber (#F59E0B) for AI agents and alerts, teal (#0D9488) for smooth operations, warm cream (#FAF9F0) background. Flat geometric style. The image should convey automated monitoring, proactive alerts, and efficient logistics. No realistic people.

## 用途说明
代表"自动数据推送、供应商管理、库存预警"的运营场景。
